Our Story

Where heritage
meets the home

Nestled in Waikkal, Terra by Shavindi brings together two worlds — the sturdy reliability of building materials and the quiet beauty of handcrafted terracotta homeware. Every piece carries the warmth of Sri Lankan earth.

We serve builders, architects, and home decorators alike, offering genuine quality with the personal touch only a family business can provide.
